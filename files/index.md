# Files & Records

This section contains compiled records, reports, transcripts, technical files, and recovered materials related to events, individuals, organizations, and systems within the Crown of Skies universe. Documents are collected, indexed, and preserved for reference, analysis, and long-term review. Some records are incomplete, damaged, classified, or subject to disagreements. 

Where possible, contextual notes have been added. Readers are advised to treat all materials as provisional representations of complex and ongoing realities.
